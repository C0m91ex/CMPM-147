Description

In this programming assignment, I built a generator that will directly read inspiring images, render parameterized images, and adjusts its own designs to optimize an evaluation score.

Technical

  1. Inspiring photos that may be chosen from a drop-down menu on the display page are integrated into this project.
  2. The design object is initialized according to the inspiration. varied inspiration choices will result in varied initDesign actions.
  3. The rendering of the design may lead to outcomes that seem connected to the motivational photos.
  4. The mutation process modifies your design bit by bit. When the rate is set to 1%, the user should be able to virtually cease exploring thanks to the use of the rate parameter. The mutation rate slider must have an effect on your generator.

Reflection

I really enjoy creating something that uses lots of fun and bold colors. Thats why I choose images that had some really bold and popping colors to really make the output of the generator really fun and interesting to look at.
